  1. The **God-breathedness** of Scripture: God speaks = God desires to be known. God writes. By what he has written, he still speaks (Heb 3:7)
  2. The **Understandableness** of Scritpure: 2 Tim 2:15 - Scripture yields to study
  3. The **Usefulness** of Scripture: 2 Tim 3:16
